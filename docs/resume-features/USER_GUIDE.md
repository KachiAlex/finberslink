## Getting Started

### What Are Resume Features?

Resume Features Completion adds powerful new capabilities to your Finbers Link resume:

- **PDF Export**: Download your resume as a professional PDF with multiple template styles
- **AI Suggestions**: Get intelligent recommendations to improve your resume content
- **Analytics**: Track who views your resume and understand engagement
- **Publishing**: Share your resume publicly for employer discovery

### Accessing Resume Features

1. Log in to your Finbers Link account
2. Navigate to "My Resumes"
3. Select a resume to edit
4. Look for the new feature buttons in the resume builder

## PDF Export

### Exporting Your Resume

#### Step 1: Click Export Button

In the resume builder, click the **"Export as PDF"** button in the top toolbar.

#### Step 2: Select Template

A dialog will appear showing three template options:

**Modern Template**
- Contemporary design with accent colors
- Sidebar layout for skills and contact info
- Best for: Tech, creative, and modern industries
- Preview: Shows sample resume in Modern style

**Classic Template**
- Traditional resume format
- Top-aligned header with contact information
- Best for: Corporate, finance, and traditional industries
- Preview: Shows sample resume in Classic style

**Minimal Template**
- Clean, simple layout
- Centered header with black and white design
- Best for: All industries, universal appeal
- Preview: Shows sample resume in Minimal style

#### Step 3: Preview Template

Click on a template to see a preview of how your resume will look. The preview shows:
- Your actual resume content formatted in the selected style
- How sections will be arranged
- Font sizes and spacing
- Color scheme (if applicable)

#### Step 4: Confirm Export

Click the **"Export"** button to generate your PDF. This may take a few seconds.

#### Step 5: Download

Once generated, a download link will appear. Click **"Download PDF"** to save the file to your computer.

The file will be named: `FirstName_LastName_Resume.pdf`

### Template Comparison

| Feature | Modern | Classic | Minimal |
|---------|--------|---------|---------|
| Design Style | Contemporary | Traditional | Simple |
| Colors | Accent colors | Black & white | Black & white |
| Layout | Sidebar | Top-aligned | Centered |
| Best For | Tech, Creative | Corporate, Finance | Universal |
| File Size | Medium | Small | Small |

### Tips for Best Results

1. **Complete Your Resume**: Ensure all sections are filled out before exporting
2. **Check Formatting**: Review the preview to ensure content displays correctly
3. **Use Consistent Formatting**: Keep bullet points and text consistent
4. **Limit Content**: Very long resumes may span multiple pages
5. **Test Download**: Try downloading on different devices to verify

### Troubleshooting Export

**Issue: Export takes too long**
- Solution: Try again in a few moments, or try a different template

**Issue: PDF looks different than preview**
- Solution: This is normal - PDFs may render slightly differently. Try a different template if needed.

**Issue: Download link doesn't work**
- Solution: Refresh the page and try exporting again

## AI Suggestions

### Getting AI Suggestions

#### Step 1: Click "Get Suggestions"

In the resume builder, click the **"Get AI Suggestions"** button. This will analyze your resume and generate improvement recommendations.

#### Step 2: Wait for Analysis

The AI will analyze your resume for:
- **Summary Clarity**: Is your professional summary clear and compelling?
- **Achievement Descriptions**: Are your achievements using the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result)?
- **Skill Relevance**: Are your skills relevant to your target roles?
- **Experience Descriptions**: Are your job descriptions detailed and impactful?

This typically takes 10-30 seconds.

#### Step 3: Review Suggestions

Once analysis completes, you'll see a list of suggestions. Each suggestion shows:

- **Category**: Type of suggestion (Summary, Achievement, Skill, Experience)
- **Confidence Level**: How confident the AI is in this suggestion (High, Medium, Low)
- **Before/After**: Original text vs. suggested improvement
- **Explanation**: Why this suggestion improves your resume

#### Step 4: Approve or Reject

For each suggestion, you can:

- **Approve**: Click the checkmark to apply the suggestion to your resume
- **Reject**: Click the X to skip this suggestion
- **Approve All**: Click "Approve All" to apply all suggestions at once
- **Reject All**: Click "Reject All" to skip all suggestions

#### Step 5: Review Changes

After approving suggestions, your resume will be updated with the changes. You can:
- Continue editing manually
- Export as PDF to see the final result
- Undo changes if needed

### Understanding Suggestions

#### Summary Suggestions

**Example**:
- **Before**: "Experienced software engineer"
- **After**: "Full-stack software engineer with 5+ years of experience building scalable web applications for Fortune 500 companies"
- **Why**: More specific and compelling summary attracts more recruiter attention

#### Achievement Suggestions

**Example**:
- **Before**: "Improved system performance"
- **After**: "Improved system performance by 40%, reducing page load time from 3.2s to 1.9s, resulting in 15% increase in user engagement"
- **Why**: Quantified achievements are more impactful and memorable

#### Skill Suggestions

**Example**:
- **Before**: "JavaScript"
- **After**: "JavaScript (ES6+, React, Node.js)"
- **Why**: Specific technologies and frameworks increase relevance for job searches

#### Experience Suggestions

**Example**:
- **Before**: "Responsible for developing features"
- **After**: "Led development of 3 major features, mentored 2 junior developers, and reduced bug count by 25%"
- **Why**: More detailed descriptions showcase leadership and impact

### Tips for Best Results

1. **Start with High Confidence**: Review suggestions with "High" confidence first
2. **Customize Suggestions**: Feel free to modify suggested text to match your voice
3. **Keep It Honest**: Only approve suggestions that accurately represent your experience
4. **Use STAR Method**: Suggestions using STAR method are more impactful
5. **Quantify Achievements**: Include numbers, percentages, and metrics when possible

### Confidence Levels Explained

- **High**: AI is very confident this suggestion improves your resume
- **Medium**: AI thinks this suggestion is helpful, but you should review carefully
- **Low**: AI suggests this might help, but it's optional

### Rate Limiting

You can request AI suggestions up to **10 times per hour**. If you exceed this limit, you'll see a message asking you to wait before requesting more suggestions.

## Analytics Dashboard

### Accessing Your Analytics

#### Step 1: Open Analytics

In the resume builder, click the **"Analytics"** tab to view engagement metrics.

#### Step 2: View Summary Metrics

The dashboard shows:

- **Total Views**: How many times your resume has been viewed
- **Total Downloads**: How many times your resume has been downloaded as PDF
- **Total Shares**: How many times your resume has been shared
- **Unique Viewers**: How many different people have viewed your resume
- **View-to-Download Ratio**: What percentage of viewers download your resume
- **Share-to-View Ratio**: What percentage of viewers share your resume

#### Step 3: Analyze Trends

View charts showing:
- **Views Over Time**: Daily/weekly/monthly view trends
- **Downloads Over Time**: Download trends
- **Shares Over Time**: Share trends

#### Step 4: Review Section Engagement

See which sections of your resume get the most attention:
- **Experience**: Most viewed section
- **Skills**: Second most viewed
- **Education**: Third most viewed
- **Projects**: Least viewed

Each section shows:
- View count
- Time spent (average seconds)
- Engagement percentage

### Using Date Range Filters

#### Preset Ranges

Click one of the preset buttons:
- **7 Days**: Last week's data
- **30 Days**: Last month's data
- **90 Days**: Last quarter's data
- **All Time**: All available data

#### Custom Date Range

1. Click "Custom Range"
2. Select start date from calendar
3. Select end date from calendar
4. Click "Apply"

The dashboard will update to show data for your selected period.

### Understanding Metrics

#### View Count

**What it means**: Total number of times your resume has been viewed

**How it's tracked**: 
- When someone opens your resume via a share link
- When someone views your published resume
- When you view your own resume (counted separately)

**Why it matters**: Higher view counts indicate more recruiter interest

#### Download Count

**What it means**: Total number of times your resume has been downloaded as PDF

**How it's tracked**: When someone clicks the download button on your resume

**Why it matters**: Downloads indicate serious interest from recruiters

#### Share Count

**What it means**: Total number of times your resume has been shared

**How it's tracked**: When someone shares your resume via email, LinkedIn, Twitter, etc.

**Why it matters**: Shares indicate your resume is being recommended to others

#### View-to-Download Ratio

**What it means**: Percentage of viewers who download your resume

**Formula**: (Downloads / Views) × 100

**Example**: 
- 100 views, 20 downloads = 20% ratio
- This means 1 in 5 viewers downloads your resume

**Why it matters**: Higher ratios indicate your resume is compelling

#### Section Engagement

**What it means**: Which sections of your resume get the most attention

**How it's tracked**: Time spent on each section, scroll depth

**Why it matters**: Helps you understand which sections are most impactful

### Viewing History

The analytics dashboard shows your **View History** table with:

- **Timestamp**: When the view occurred
- **Device**: Desktop, mobile, or tablet
- **Browser**: Chrome, Safari, Firefox, etc.
- **Location**: Country and city (if available)
- **Time Spent**: How long they viewed your resume

Views are listed in reverse chronological order (most recent first).

### Exporting Analytics

#### Export as CSV

1. Click "Export" button
2. Select "CSV Format"
3. Choose date range
4. Click "Download"

CSV file includes all analytics data in spreadsheet format.

#### Export as PDF

1. Click "Export" button
2. Select "PDF Format"
3. Choose date range
4. Click "Download"

PDF file includes formatted report with charts and summary.

### Tips for Analyzing Analytics

1. **Track Trends**: Monitor how views change over time
2. **Identify Peak Times**: See when your resume gets the most views
3. **Optimize Sections**: Focus on improving sections with low engagement
4. **Compare Periods**: Compare week-over-week or month-over-month changes
5. **Export Reports**: Save reports for your records

## Publishing Your Resume

### Making Your Resume Public

#### Step 1: Open Publishing Settings

In the resume builder, click the **"Settings"** tab and scroll to "Publishing".

#### Step 2: Enable Publishing

Click the **"Publish Resume"** toggle to make your resume publicly discoverable.

#### Step 3: Copy Public URL

Your resume now has a unique public URL. Click **"Copy to Clipboard"** to copy the link.

The URL format is: `https://finberslink.com/public/resumes/{unique-id}`

#### Step 4: Share Your Resume

Share your public URL with:
- Recruiters
- Employers
- Networking contacts
- Social media
- Email signature

### What Happens When You Publish

When you publish your resume:

1. **Public Access**: Anyone with the link can view your resume without logging in
2. **Discovery Index**: Your resume is added to the employer discovery index
3. **View Tracking**: Views to your public resume are tracked in analytics
4. **Public Profile**: Your resume appears in search results when employers search

### What Employers See

When an employer views your published resume, they see:

- Your complete resume content
- Your name and contact information
- Publication date
- View count
- No edit options (read-only)

### Unpublishing Your Resume

To make your resume private again:

1. Open Publishing Settings
2. Click the **"Publish Resume"** toggle to turn it off
3. Confirm unpublishing

When you unpublish:
- Resume is removed from public access
- URL no longer works
- Resume is removed from discovery index
- Analytics continue to be tracked

### Privacy and Security

**Your Information is Safe**:
- Only published resumes are publicly accessible
- Unpublished resumes are completely private
- You control who can see your resume
- Your email and phone are only visible to you

**Best Practices**:
- Only publish when actively job searching
- Unpublish when you find a job
- Review your resume before publishing
- Keep contact information current

### Searching for Published Resumes

Employers can search for published resumes by:

- **Keywords**: Job title, skills, experience
- **Skills**: Filter by specific skills
- **Target Roles**: Filter by desired positions
- **Industries**: Filter by industry

Your resume appears in search results if it matches the search criteria.

### Tips for Getting Discovered

1. **Complete Your Resume**: Fill out all sections
2. **Use Keywords**: Include relevant keywords in your summary and experience
3. **List Skills**: Add all relevant skills
4. **Specify Target Roles**: Clearly state your target positions
5. **Keep Updated**: Update your resume regularly
6. **Share Actively**: Share your public URL on social media and with contacts

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Are these features free?**
A: Yes, all Resume Features are included with your Finbers Link account at no additional cost.

**Q: Can I use multiple templates?**
A: Yes, you can export your resume with different templates to see which style you prefer.

**Q: How long does PDF export take?**
A: Usually 5-10 seconds. If it takes longer, try again.

**Q: Can I edit my resume after exporting?**
A: Yes, the PDF export doesn't affect your resume. You can continue editing and export again.

### PDF Export Questions

**Q: What file format is the PDF?**
A: Standard PDF format compatible with all PDF readers.

**Q: Can I customize the template?**
A: Currently, templates are fixed. You can edit your resume content, but not the template design.

**Q: What if my resume is very long?**
A: Long resumes will span multiple pages. The PDF will include all content with proper pagination.

**Q: Can I include my photo in the PDF?**
A: Yes, if you have a profile photo, you can include it in the PDF export.

### AI Suggestions Questions

**Q: How does the AI work?**
A: The AI analyzes your resume content and compares it against best practices for resume writing.

**Q: Can I trust the AI suggestions?**
A: AI suggestions are recommendations. Always review and customize them to match your experience and voice.

**Q: What if I don't like the suggestions?**
A: You can reject any suggestions. Only approve changes you're comfortable with.

**Q: How often can I request suggestions?**
A: You can request suggestions up to 10 times per hour.

**Q: Will suggestions change my resume without permission?**
A: No, you must explicitly approve each suggestion before it's applied.

### Analytics Questions

**Q: How is my resume view tracked?**
A: Views are tracked when someone opens your resume via a share link or public URL.

**Q: Does my own viewing count?**
A: Your own views are tracked separately and not included in the main view count.

**Q: How accurate is the location data?**
A: Location is determined from IP address geolocation, which is usually accurate to the city level.

**Q: How long is analytics data kept?**
A: Analytics data is kept for at least 1 year.

**Q: Can I delete analytics data?**
A: You can request to delete analytics data, but it's kept for compliance purposes.

### Publishing Questions

**Q: Is my published resume secure?**
A: Yes, your published resume is read-only. Viewers cannot edit or download it without permission.

**Q: Can I unpublish my resume later?**
A: Yes, you can unpublish at any time. The public URL will no longer work.

**Q: Who can see my published resume?**
A: Anyone with the public URL can view it. It's also searchable in the employer discovery index.

**Q: Can I have multiple published resumes?**
A: Yes, you can publish multiple resumes for different job targets.

**Q: What happens to my analytics when I unpublish?**
A: Analytics data is preserved. You can view historical data even after unpublishing.

### Troubleshooting Questions

**Q: PDF export is not working. What should I do?**
A: Try refreshing the page and exporting again. If the problem persists, contact support.

**Q: AI suggestions are taking too long. What should I do?**
A: Wait a moment and try again. If it continues to fail, try later.

**Q: Analytics dashboard is not loading. What should I do?**
A: Try refreshing the page. If the problem persists, clear your browser cache and try again.

**Q: My published resume is not appearing in search results. What should I do?**
A: Ensure your resume is published and contains relevant keywords. It may take a few hours to appear in search results.

### Account Questions

**Q: Can I export my resume data?**
A: Yes, you can export your resume as PDF or CSV.

**Q: Can I delete my resume?**
A: Yes, you can delete your resume from the "My Resumes" page. This will also delete associated analytics.

**Q: Can I recover a deleted resume?**
A: Deleted resumes cannot be recovered. Please contact support if you need assistance.

**Q: How do I change my resume?**
A: Click "Edit" on any resume to make changes. Changes are saved automatically.
