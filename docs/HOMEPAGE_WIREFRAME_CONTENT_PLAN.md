# Homepage Wireframe Content Plan

This wireframe aligns the homepage structure with clearer content for Finbers Link's target market.

## Desktop Wireframe

### 1. Hero
- Label: Career Intelligence Platform
- Headline: Build skills, prove readiness, and get hired.
- Supporting copy: Explain that learning, profile building, and job matching are in one workflow.
- Primary CTA: Start your career route
- Secondary CTA: Explore opportunities
- Right panel: Hiring readiness score with route progress

### 2. Message Strip
- Line 1: Welcome to the future of career navigation
- Line 2: Clear steps + Practical outcomes

### 3. Quick Actions
- Start with learning
- See matched roles
- Build your profile

### 4. Platform Features
- Integrated LMS
- Community Feed and Chat
- Smart Job Board
- Electronic Profile Resume
- AI Skills Gap Identifier
- Labor Market Intelligence

### 5. Audience Fit
- Students and career switchers
- Training institutions and workforce programs
- Employers and hiring teams
- Governments and global professionals

### 6. Global Positioning
- Explain international and multi-sector relevance
- Highlight practical, demand-backed career navigation

### 7. How It Works (4 Steps)
- Step 1: Identify where you are
- Step 2: Identify skill gaps
- Step 3: Get a smart route
- Step 4: Apply where you qualify
- Include a route snapshot card with next actions and matched roles

### 8. Why It Matters
- Pain points: random courses, blind applications, unclear readiness
- Value outcomes:
  - Know what to fix
  - Know what to learn
  - Know where to apply
  - Know when you are ready

### 9. Institutional CTA
- Encourage programs and employers to onboard
- CTA: Talk to the team

## Mobile Wireframe

### 1. Hero stack
- Label, headline, support copy, CTAs, readiness panel

### 2. Message strip
- Keep short, one line per message

### 3. Quick actions
- Render as stacked cards

### 4. Features
- Single-column cards in priority order

### 5. Audience fit
- Stacked cards with short descriptors

### 6. How it works
- Vertical 4-step timeline cards

### 7. Why it matters
- Pain points card then outcomes card

### 8. Final CTA
- Full-width button: Talk to the team
